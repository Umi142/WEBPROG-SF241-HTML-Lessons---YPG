The Cat

The cat is the ruler of its cozy little kingdom at home.

Its world can be divided into two parts: the playful side and the sleepy side. The playful side is centered around toys and mischief, while the sleepy side is the calm core where naps are taken and dreams are made.

The cat usually rests along the warmest spot of the house, claiming it as its territory.

Facts:

  • Cats believe every cardboard box is a luxury condo
  • They think 3 a.m. zoomies are a perfectly reasonable fitness routine
  • A cat’s definition of “helping” is sitting directly on your keyboard
  • Cats rub themselves on a person to show affection.
